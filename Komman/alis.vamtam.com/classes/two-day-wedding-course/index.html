Two Day Wedding Course

October 31, 2023 @ 8:00 am - December 19, 2024 @ 9:00 am

Free
  • « The Wedding Planning Institute

Event
description

Bells are ringing! A wedding announcement can cause alarm bells to ring for many people. Our two day course will allay all your fears, especially when it comes to bridal flowers.

Bridal flowers: Where does one start? What does one use? Is the DIY route really the correct one to take? Over this exciting pair of days, you will be taught the technical skills needed to deal with all aspects of creating traditional and contemporary wedding designs.

You will gain an understanding of the essential basics of bridal flowers, which includes their conditioning, their careful selection, the seasons and the process of costing to a budget and the various stages that are involved in designing someone's (or your own) wedding flowers.

Over two days, you will-create-a range of bouquets, posies, buttonholes and corsages in a variety of styles. It's a fun, educational and deeply romantic course, and of interest to both the complete beginner and the more advanced student.

Flowers can transform a wedding and we are proud to tell you that this course will help you achieve the fabulous results that go into a perfect day.
